The second part of the ATCG project. A publication that translates Heukels' *Flora van Nederland* — the standard reference for Dutch plant taxonomy — into DNA colour codes. Each plant family becomes a bar of colour, a genetic fingerprint made visible. The book is both a scientific document and a visual score.
