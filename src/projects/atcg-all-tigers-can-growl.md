*"Red. Green. Blue. Black. Green. …"* From behind the leaves, voices repeatedly whisper, shout and chatter four colours in different sequences. The title refers to Adenine, Thymine, Cytosine and Guanine — the four bases in DNA — and their commonly used mnemonic: All Tigers Can Growl.

Through the project *Kingdoms of Life Being Barcoded*, all DNA-codes are being collected, identified and visualised as colour-based bar codes. In her sound installation and book, Lyske Gais announces all fifteen hundred Dutch plant families, drawing from Heukels' *Flora van Nederland*.

The work brings together botany, genetics and language: the naming of plants, the coding of life, the colour of sound.
